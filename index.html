Geo War Game Rules

Game Overview

In this strategy game, players bid on countries, battle for control, and manage their resources, including money and military power (MP), to conquer the world. The goal is to acquire as many countries as possible while using strategy, diplomacy, and resources wisely.

Game Setup

  • Players: 4 players, each starting with 3 billion dollars.
  • Maximum MP: Each player can have a maximum of 125 MP (military power).

Special Conditions

Recovering Countries

Definition: Recovering countries are countries that are in the process of recovering from negative events (like war, economic collapse, etc.).

Recovery Process:

  • Mark the recovery time by placing tokens (or checker pieces) on the country card. Recovery time varies depending on the intensity of the event.
  • If a player attacks a recovering country, the recovering country loses 15 MP in the battle.
  • Recovering countries cannot attack or give payouts.

The Auction

Auction Process

  • Countries are revealed individually and auctioned off to the players.
  • If no one bids for a country, its price is halved, and the auction continues until the country is bought.

Selling Countries

  • On a player’s turn, they may sell one of their countries to another player or the bank.
  • If selling to the bank, the player receives the starting bid price, and the country is auctioned again.
  • The player who sells the country cannot repurchase it.

Attacking

General Attack Rules

  • Players may attack as many countries as they like per turn, provided they meet the attack requirements.
  • To attack a country, the player must:
    • Answer 1 trivia question correctly.
    • Answer 2 trivia questions correctly if attacking an island nation.
  • If the attacker is an island nation, they must answer only 1 trivia question correctly if attacking another island nation.
  • The country you attack must be bordering your country. If you fail to answer the preparation trivia question, pay 10m and continue the attack.

Starting the Battle

  • Any player asks a trivia question to the defender and attacker.
  • The first player to answer correctly gains a +10 MP bonus.
  • Incorrect answers result in a -5 MP penalty.

Special Actions During Battle

  • Diplomatic Solution: The attacker can propose a diplomatic solution, where the defender agrees to end the battle. The attacker pays a 10 million dollar fee and leaves the country.
  • Calling for Resources: The attacker can request resources from their other countries or from other players. Each resource received adds +5 MP, but the country sending resources goes into recovery for 1 turn.

Battle Outcome

If the Attacker Wins:

  • The attacker collects the bid price of the country and gains control of the country card.
  • The defender’s country goes into recovery for 3 turns.
  • Recovery time for the attacking country is calculated based on the difference in MP:
    • Win by < 15 MP: 3 turns recovery.
    • Win by < 30 MP: 2 turns recovery.
    • Win by < 100 MP: 1 turn recovery.

If the Defender Wins:

  • The attacker must pay 10 million to the defender and 10 million to the bank.
  • Both players keep their countries.
  • Both countries go into recovery for 2 turns.

Payout

At the end of each player's turn, they receive money for each country they own, excluding recovering countries.

Payout Example: If a player owns 10 countries, but 2 of them are recovering, they will receive 8 million dollars (1 million per country).

Special Cards

Limited Edition! only 16 special cards are available in the game! One-time use per game!

Purchasing Special Cards:

  • Players receive special cards if they conquer a country on their turn.

Special Cards:

These cards offer powerful one-time effects that can be used during the game, but remember—once used, they’re gone! After using your special card, set it aside from the main game, not to be used again..

Structure of a Player’s Turn

  • Attack as many countries as desired (optional).
  • Purchase Special Cards (optional).
  • Remove one turn from any recovering countries (if applicable).
  • Collect Payout based on the countries owned.
